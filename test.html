EDUCATION

Besides exhibition projects, education is one of the basic activities of the Museum. The main purpose of conducting guided tours, lectures and workshops is to complete the knowledge of the participants on specific topics as well as on the mission and activities of the Museum, museum collections, items etc. Besides guided tours and lectures, from 2019 onwards Museum also offers a set of workshops on topics related to Museums' temporary and permanent exhibitions and the Castle itself.
Find out more by clicking on one of the options below.

1. Guided tours

Museum workers conduct guided tours for groups of visitors of all ages (min. 10 visitors) upon previous announcements.

Duration ca. 45 min.

Price: according to Odluka.

Implementation: January – December, with previous announcement.

2. Museum workshops

I, THE KNIGHT!

Description:
Upon arrival at the Castle a short guided tour will be conducted with the emphasis on the concept of the Castle, its historical overview and importance, architectural characteristics and function, the Pazin County and the exhibition Pazin Castle and the Serfs' Uprising in the 15th and 16th century. The participants are guided towards answering the following questions: What was the Pazin County?, What were the uprisings?, Why did the uprisings occur and how did they end?, Which were the weapons used? etc. The introduction is followed by a workshop during which the participants make their own spear based on the exhibition replicas.

Goals:
  • familiarisation with the history of the Castle, ways, conditions and reasons of building,
  • familiarisation with the relevant concepts (castle, feudalism, Pazin County, the Count, uprising of the serfs, Knight, spear, …)
Age: primary school; possibility of customization for secondary school, adults etc. (with previous announcement and arrangement).

Type:
  • regular, for domestic and foreign visitors,
  • for organized arrivals with guidance with the emphasis on the importance and the architecture of the Castle and the Pazin Castle and the Serfs' Uprising in the 15th and 16th century,
  • can be implemented in schools – the initial lecture held at school is followed by a visit to the Museum and a workshop.
Duration: guided tour or lecture ca. 15 minutes, workshop ca. 20 minutes.

Price: according to the Odluka.

Implementation: May – September with previous announcement, June – August, every Thursday from 11 AM – 2 PM

MY COAT OF ARMS

Description:
A short introductory guided tour includes familiarisation with the history of the Pazin Castle, historical facts of the Pazin County and important families who administered over the County as well as with basic concepts and rules of Heraldry. The introductory part is followed by a workshop during which the participants will make their own coats of arms based on the example of those on the facade of the Castle. The coats of arms can also be made including the participants' names or family names. VAŽNO: Preparation by the students is required – information of their name and family name, its meaning or history.

Goals:
  • familiarisation with the term and basic rules of Heraldry,
  • familiarisation with the coats of arms on the facade of the Pazin Castle – important families, history of the Castle and the Pazin County,
  • familiarisation with the history of Pazin and the surrounding area (possible adaptation to other towns and places and their history).
Age: primary school; possibility of customization for secondary school, adults etc. (with previous announcement and arrangement).

Type:
  • regular, for domestic and foreign visitors,
  • for organized arrivals with guidance with the emphasis on the coats of arms at the facade of the Pazin Castle (or related with the history of other places)
  • can be implemented in schools – the initial lecture held at school is followed by a visit to the Museum and a workshop.
Duration: guided tour or lecture ca. 15 minutes, workshop ca. 25 minutes

Price: According to Odluka.

Implementation: Participants are divided into groups (min. 2 groups, max. one school grade divided into 4-5 groups) and given the instructions at the entrance. Each groups gets the materials needed (pen, paper) for problem solving. Tasks (mathematic, logical and orientation) are printed on notes and hidden all over the Castle and Museums' permanent and temporary exhibitions. Each note contains a question leading to the other, i.e. one trail leads to the other until the participants reach the hidden Treasure. The group which finds the Treasure first wins.

Description:

Each groups gets the materials needed (pen, paper) for problem solving. Tasks (mathematic, logical and orientation) are printed on notes and hidden all over the Castle and Museums' permanent and temporary exhibitions. primary school; possibility of customization for secondary school, adults etc. (with previous announcement and arrangement).

  • regular, for domestic and foreign visitors,

3. Lectures

In cooperation with teachers and professors of primary and secondary schools, Pazin City Museum can organize and conduct a (series) of lectures on certain topics relevant to the thematic units and school curriculum, related with the fields of interest of the Museum.
